# Markdown++ Brand Assets

## Logo: Hashmark Monogram

The Markdown++ logo is a **Hashmark Monogram** -- two geometric `+` signs offset diagonally so their combined strokes also read as a `#` (hash/octothorpe).

- **Primary reading:** `++` (increment, additive extension)
- **Secondary discovery:** `#` (Markdown's heading character)
- **Design principle:** Like the FedEx arrow -- once you see it, you can't unsee it

## Usage Guidelines

### Minimum size

The mark is legible down to 16x16 pixels. Use the favicon-optimized SVG (`favicon.svg`) for sizes 32px and below.

### Clear space

Maintain clear space around the mark equal to at least 25% of the mark's height on all sides.

### Color

- **Default:** `#1a1a1a` (near-black) on light backgrounds
- **Dark mode:** `#ffffff` (white) on dark backgrounds
- **Monochrome:** `#000000` (pure black) for print
- **Accent:** `#4f46e5` (indigo) for branded contexts

### Don'ts

- Don't rotate or skew the mark
- Don't add drop shadows or gradients
- Don't change the proportions or stroke weight
- Don't place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
